This extension allows you to download videos, images, and carousels directly from posts, supporting batch downloads and ZIP packaging.

Features:
- Batch download videos and images
- Support ZIP packaging
- Multi-language support
- Privacy-friendly, all processing is local and no data is uploaded
- All processing is done locally for enhanced security


How it works
- When you hover over media, a save button appears at the bottom left
- Adds a download menu to all posts containing media
